Chandigarh Amusement Park

Client: Government of Punjab / Private Consortium

Location: Chandigarh, India

Project Type: Recreational & Leisure Development

Project Status: Completed

Design Firm: PSA Studio

Project Architect: Mukta Rai

Professional Contribution by Mukta Rai – Project Architect

As the Project Architect at PSA Studio, I was entrusted with the end-to-end architectural leadership and execution of the Chandigarh Amusement Park, a landmark recreational development aimed at redefining public leisure experiences in the region. My role spanned from conceptual design through detailed documentation and coordination with multidisciplinary consultants, ensuring the seamless realization of a vibrant, sustainable, and user-centric environment.

Key Responsibilities & Contributions

  • Design Leadership: Spearheaded the architectural design process, translating the client’s vision into a cohesive, imaginative, and functional layout. Developed the master planning framework, zoning strategy, and spatial experience journey, balancing thrill zones, family-friendly areas, green spaces, and service infrastructure.
  • Concept Development: Led the design team in generating innovative architectural concepts inspired by Chandigarh’s modernist heritage and regional context, integrating playful geometries, dynamic façades, and interactive elements to reflect the spirit of an amusement destination.
  • Technical Documentation: Oversaw the production of comprehensive construction drawings, architectural details, and specifications. Ensured compliance with NBC, fire safety norms, accessibility standards (CPWD), and municipal regulations throughout all project phases.
  • Stakeholder Coordination: Acted as the primary point of contact between the client, consultants (structural, MEP, landscape, signage), and local authorities. Facilitated design reviews, coordinated approvals, and managed feedback integration to maintain design integrity and project timelines.
  • Sustainability Integration: Championed eco-sensitive design strategies, including passive cooling, rainwater harvesting, solar-responsive orientation, and use of locally sourced, low-impact materials—aligning the project with sustainable development goals.
  • Construction Supervision: Conducted site visits and coordinated with the construction team to ensure accurate translation of design intent, resolve on-site challenges, and maintain quality control across all built elements.
  • Team Management: Mentored and guided a team of junior architects and designers, fostering a collaborative studio culture and ensuring timely delivery of design milestones.

The Chandigarh Amusement Park stands as a testament to innovative leisure architecture, combining experiential design with operational efficiency. My contribution as Project Architect was instrumental in shaping a destination that not only meets the recreational needs of diverse user groups but also sets a new benchmark for public entertainment infrastructure in North India.

Mukta Rai
