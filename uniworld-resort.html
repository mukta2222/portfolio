UNIWORLD RESORTS

Gurugram, Haryana

Project Highlights

Luxury Villa Development

An expansive 86-acre gated community featuring approximately 160 high-end villas designed for a sophisticated, resort-style living experience. [8, 9]

Varied & Adaptable Designs

Two distinct plot sizes (420 sq. m. and 840 sq. m.) each offer two unique villa designs, allowing for conventional front-facing or private rear-facing gardens. [9]

Resort-Style Amenities

Large villas feature private lap pools, while smaller villas are equipped with outdoor jacuzzis on the terrace, enhancing the luxurious, leisure-focused ambiance. [9, 12, 18]

Architectural Harmony

The design incorporates thoughtfully crafted sloping terracotta roofs and a modern material palette to create a cohesive and elegant resort-like atmosphere. [7]

Architectural Role

  • Led the architectural design from conceptualization to execution, focusing on integrating modern aesthetics with functional resort-style living.
  • Developed two distinct villa typologies and site layouts to create varied streetscapes and cater to different homeowner preferences for privacy and openness.
  • Orchestrated the material and textural palette, blending stone cladding, wood elements, and stucco to articulate the geometric forms and create a warm, contemporary facade.
  • Coordinated with structural and landscape teams to ensure seamless integration of building design with private amenities like lap pools, jacuzzis, and gardens.
  • Supervised the construction phase, ensuring design intent, material quality, and high-end finishes were maintained throughout the project execution. [6]
