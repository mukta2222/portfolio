Indiabulls Enigma

Project Address: Sector 110, Gurgaon

Project Highlights

  • Innovative Architectural Layout: The unique cross-shaped master plan ensures no apartment directly overlooks another, maximizing privacy while promoting exceptional cross-ventilation and penetration of natural light throughout the living spaces.
  • Expansive Luxury Residences: Offering generously sized 4 & 5-BHK apartments and penthouses, the project caters to a premium lifestyle with floor plans ranging from 3,350 to 6,790 sq. ft., designed for spatial comfort and elegance.
  • Integrated Green Spaces: Over 16 acres of the site are dedicated to themed landscapes and verdant buffers, creating a sound-insulated and ecologically balanced environment that integrates nature with modern living.
  • Premium Material Palette: The interiors are defined by a commitment to quality, featuring high-end materials like imported marble flooring, elegant teakwood door frames, and superior modern fixtures for a lasting sense of luxury.
  • World-Class Amenities: A state-of-the-art clubhouse, swimming pool, and comprehensive sports facilities are seamlessly integrated into the design, providing residents with a holistic and active lifestyle.
  • Strategic Urban Connectivity: Located in the prime corridor of Sector 110 on the Dwarka Expressway, the project offers unparalleled access to Delhi, Gurgaon's commercial hubs, and the IGI Airport.

Architectural Role

Design Conceptualization

Contributed to the core architectural concept, focusing on translating the "imagination engineering" vision into functional unit layouts and ensuring the spatial hierarchy achieved the desired balance of privacy and community.

Material & Aesthetic Curation

Spearheaded the selection of the interior and exterior material palette. My focus was on curating durable, high-end finishes that would elevate the aesthetic and align with the project's luxurious positioning.

Interdisciplinary Coordination

Acted as a crucial liaison between the lead design firm (ARCOP) and specialized consultants, including landscape designers, to ensure cohesive integration of all architectural and environmental elements.
