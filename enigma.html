Enigma, India Bulls Group Housing

Premium Residential Towers | Dwarka Expressway, Gurugram | Completed 2022

Project Overview

Indiabulls Enigma is a landmark of high-rise luxury residential architecture strategically located on the Dwarka Expressway in Gurugram. [4] This development emphasizes opulent living through meticulously designed 4 and 5 BHK apartments and penthouses, where form and function converge to create an exceptional living experience. [3, 2] The project integrates eco-friendly design, Vaastu principles, and state-of-the-art sustainable systems, offering residents a harmonious blend of modern comforts and natural tranquility with extensive landscaped greens and premium lifestyle amenities. [3, 2]

Architectural Contributions

  • Played a key role in the initial design conceptualization workshops with the lead architect (ARCOP) and client, focusing on creating a landmark elevation and luxurious user experience. [2]
  • Contributed to the spatial planning of unit layouts, focusing on optimizing natural light, cross-ventilation, and ensuring that apartments do not overlook one another for enhanced privacy. [2, 6]
  • Involved in the research and integration of sustainable building practices and specifications for an earthquake-resistant RCC framed structure, aligning with modern construction standards. [3]
  • Facilitated on-site architectural coordination with engineering teams to ensure design integrity was maintained from foundation to finishing stages.
  • Actively participated in design review meetings with clients and consultant teams, helping refine material selections like imported marble, teakwood frames, and modular kitchen specifications. [3, 4]
  • Assisted in developing the site-level master plan, including the strategic placement of towers, clubhouse, green buffer zones, and recreational areas to foster a community-centric environment. [2]
