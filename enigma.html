Indiabulls Enigma

Sector 110, Gurgaon

Project Highlights

  • Innovative Design for Privacy: The township's layout, designed by the renowned firm ARCOP, ensures that no apartment overlooks another. This unique design principle maximizes natural light and cross-ventilation for all residences.
  • Luxury and Eco-Friendly Architecture: The project features spacious 4 and 5-BHK apartments and penthouses with high ceilings, imported marble, and teakwood frames.It also incorporates eco-friendly architectural principles.
  • Expansive Green Spaces: Spread across approximately 19.85 acres, the development boasts extensive themed landscapes that create a natural green buffer and a serene environment.
  • Comprehensive Modern Amenities: Residents have access to a wide array of facilities, including a clubhouse with a spa and gym, swimming pools, sports courts, and children's play areas.
  • Strategic and Vaastu-Compliant Planning: The master plan is a blend of aesthetics and technology, with a focus on Vaastu-compliant architecture to enhance the living experience.The location on Dwarka Expressway provides excellent connectivity.

My Contributions

Design Conceptualization & Development

I was instrumental in the early-stage design process and I was fully involved in the project from inception to completion, overseeing design development, working and submission drawings, BOQ, tender documentation, and site supervision..

Material and Finish Selection

My role involved curating the material palette for both interior and exterior applications. I focused on selecting premium and sustainable materials such as the imported marble and teakwood, ensuring that the final aesthetic aligned with the project's luxury positioning while adhering to eco-friendly standards.

Stakeholder Coordination & Project Management

I facilitated seamless communication between the design team, engineering, and landscaping consultants from Site Concepts. This ensured that the architectural vision was executed precisely and that the integration of the built form with the landscape was harmonious and effective.
