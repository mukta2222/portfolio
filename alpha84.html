AlphaCorp GurgaonOne

Sector 84, Gurgaon, Haryana

Key Features

Expansive Green Spaces

Over 90% of the project area is dedicated to open and green landscapes, fostering a connection with nature.

Strategic Master Planning

Designed by renowned Canadian architects ARCOP, the seven towers are positioned to maximize openness and views.

Sustainable Living

Incorporates eco-friendly features like water conservation, waste management, and sustainable energy practices.

Modern Amenities

Includes a recreational club, swimming pool, sports courts, and facilities for a well-rounded lifestyle.

My Contributions

  • Led the conceptual design phase, translating the vision of a nature-integrated living space into architectural plans.
  • Managed the coordination between structural engineers, landscape designers, and construction teams to ensure design integrity.
  • Developed spatial planning for the residential units, focusing on creating functional, three-side open apartments for optimal light and ventilation.
  • Oversaw the selection of the material palette, balancing aesthetics with durability and sustainability goals.
  • Ensured project compliance with local building codes and standards while integrating innovative design solutions.
